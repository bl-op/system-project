Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Cari Fund is exceptionally responsible to our thousands and millions of donators and supporters in securing a safer donation to accomodate the donations for a better future of the people in need. With your help, we are helping millions of people to live better lives together in this world. Get answers to our frequently asked questions about donating to the Cari Fund.


Donation Questions

Can I donate through money or cheques?

In Cari Fund, there are two methods of donating:

  • Bank Transfer
  • Cheque or cash
Bank Transfer
A considerable and trouble-free way to make donations is through Bank Transfer. Depending on different fundraiser oganization, you may refer to the specific fundraiser organization information regarding their Bank Name and Account Number in order to make donation.

Cheque or cash
If donating via cheque, you are required to make payable to: Cari Fund Sdn Bhd by including these information:
  1. Fundraiser name
  2. Category
  3. Amount specified

Do I need to create an account to make donation?

You do not. Only fundraiser organization are required to sign up to ensure that proceeds are received and donated safely by the end of the day.

You may choose to donate anonymously by clicking on a project and select Donate Now.

How much can I donate?

In Cari Fund, there is no limit to make donations. This means you can make different amount of donations from as low as RM0.01 and as large as you wish!

These funds will be served as a paramount contribution for the people in need.

Where will my funds go after donating?

Proceeds will directly be allocated to each of our fundraiser organization's account with multiple subcategories. This ensures that all donation is sorted accordingly and no donation will interfere with other different categories.


Security Questions

How do I know a certain fundraiser or organization is genuinely raising funds?

If you approach an organization that claims to be raising funds for Cari Fund and you think they may not be a legitimate fundraiser, you may contact the Cari Fund Support team through our Helpline directory 03-12345678 or email directly to us at support@carifund.org.my with their information.

Cari Fund strictly ensure that all fundraiser organizations are genuine and undergone validation and verification to proceed with their projects.

Who from Cari Fund might contact me?

If you have recently made donation, Cari Fund will send a confirmation text message and an email.

If you have donated anonymously, you will only be issued with a receipt to which you can print for any verification or clarification issue.
