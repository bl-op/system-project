Who are we?

Cari Fund is a renown global crowdfunding community dedicated towards addressing and catering the welfare of the ones in need.

Cari Fund is a non-profit organization that engages donors from different countries around the world. This organization represents an instrumental figure in providing various tools and assistance for donors and the public to create projects and make safe donations.
